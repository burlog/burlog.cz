Privacy Policy — Marsovský PS

App: cz.burlog.tabor.mps · Last updated: 2026-06-24

This Privacy Policy explains how the Marsovský PS application (“the App”) handles your information. The App is provided by the developer (“we”, “us”).

Summary

The App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device. The App has no internet access.

Information the App accesses

Location

The App uses your device’s GPS location (the ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION permissions) for a single purpose: to read your current position and convert it, entirely on your device, into the App’s in-game “Mars” coordinate display.

  • Your location is used only in memory while the App is open and is shown on screen.
  • Your location is never stored on the device, saved to a file, sent over the internet, or shared with us or any third party.
  • The App requests location updates only while it is in the foreground and stops them when it is paused or closed.

You can deny or revoke the location permission at any time in your device settings. Without location permission, the coordinate display will be unavailable, but the rest of the App continues to work.

Information we do NOT collect

The App does not collect or process any of the following: name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, files, device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, or crash data. The App contains no advertising and no third-party SDKs.

Network access

The App does not request the INTERNET permission and makes no network connections. No data leaves your device.

Children’s privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Data retention and deletion

Because the App stores no personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Uninstalling the App removes it completely from your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App and/or this page.
